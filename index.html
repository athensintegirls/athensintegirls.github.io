Our Mission

As girls and non-binary individuals in math, all of us have seen the stark differences between the number of boys, girls, and non-binary participants in traditional math competitions or math-related activities in general. This monumental gap has discouraged many girls and non-binary people around the world from pursuing math, either as a hobby or a career. Through our competition, we hope to inspire a passion for this subject in more girls and non-binary individuals, helping to close this gap. Additionally, by bringing together those who love math, we aim to create a community of future female and non-binary mathematicians who can support each other in the face of any discrimination. Math is an amazing subject full of hidden puzzles and strategies, and together, we want to create an event full of joy where girls and non-binary participants can bond over the beauty of the subject.
