Welcome to INTEGIRLS Athens, Greece!

Our Mission

INTEGIRLS is a nonprofit teen-led organization with a mission to bridge the gender gap in STEM through exciting math opportunities! All our programs are offered free of cost to our participants.

Upcoming Events

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

I’m interested but have no math competition experience. Can I still attend?


Of course, you should really at eventtend this competition. In the past, many people have stated that INTEGIRLS gave them an entry into math competitions and they had no idea how it was until they attended the event. If you would like any pointers to understand how to prepare, you can feel free to email us. Hope to see you at our events.

I do not have a INTEGIRLS Chapter near my area, but I am interested in participating in the competition. Can I still attend?


We also encourage students not only from Athens to attend, but also other areas as well. You may reach out to us at the links provided at the bottom of this page.

Who can I contact if I have any questions?


You can contact us through Email or Instagram at the links provided at the bottom of this page. If it is urgent, you can message us on Instagram or add "URGENT" is subject line of your email.

I do not identify as female, but I would like to participate. How can I do that?


If you do not identify as female or non-binary, we encourage you to check out other opportunities we may share. You can contact la@integirls.org to find more activities apt for you.

How can I contribute?


You may choose to volunteer for us by emailing us at athens@integirls.org or can donate to our non-profit here. You can also email us at la@integirls.org to receive more information about how you can help. YOUR HELP MAKES A DIFFERENCE TO US.
